About Nexvolt Systems

Nexvolt Systems is a hardware-focused technology venture working on next-generation touch-controlled electrical interfaces.

We prioritize electrical correctness, isolation, and long-term reliability over unnecessary features and software dependency.

Touch Switch Platform

The Nexvolt touch switch platform replaces conventional mechanical switches using capacitive touch sensing combined with isolated load control electronics.

The system is intended for integration behind acrylic or glass panels while maintaining electrical safety and deterministic behavior.

Technology

  • Capacitive touch sensing interface
  • Isolated low-voltage control domain
  • Noise-resistant PCB layout methodology
  • Designed for Indian mains voltage conditions
  • Fail-safe output behavior

Development Status

  • Touch sensing architecture finalized
  • Electrode geometry under optimization
  • LED indication and optical routing in progress
  • PCB layout refinement ongoing
  • Mechanical integration planning underway

Design Philosophy

Nexvolt Systems follows a hardware-first design methodology. Core functionality does not depend on connectivity or software services.

A product is considered complete only when it is electrically sound, mechanically stable, and reproducible at scale.
